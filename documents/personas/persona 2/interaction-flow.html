Interaction Flow

  • Maggie opens the Vaultify web application on her laptop.
  • Vaultify automatically logs her in with her saved credentials.
  • Maggie clicks on the Upload Receipt button on the dashboard.
  • Vaultify opens a pop-up window to select an image or take a photo.
  • Maggie selects a photo of her Costco receipt from her computer.
  • Vaultify scans and uploads the image, extracting the store name, date, and total amount.
  • Maggie adds a note saying “Groceries – Costco” and clicks Save Receipt.
  • Vaultify confirms with a message “Receipt Saved Successfully!”
  • Maggie clicks on Monthly Summary from the sidebar.
  • Vaultify displays a bar chart of her spending for October, showing total spent at Costco and local stores.
  • Maggie smiles, realizing she stayed within her monthly grocery budget.
  • She closes the app, confident that her receipts are safely stored for future reference.
