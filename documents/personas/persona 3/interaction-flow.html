Home Persona User Story

Interaction Flow

Use Case

Angela is searching for a way to keep business expenses and receipts organized and tracked. She does a search and finds our webpage/app.

Precondition

An Internet search for which online tool will be suit her needs.

Postcondition

Angela decides that our website/application will suit her needs and creates and uses an account.

Interaction Flow

  • Angela arrives at our website after a search helps her determine that it will work for her needs
  • She does some reading on the home page, then decides to sign up
  • She clicks the "Sign Up" button on our home page
  • The Sign Up button takes her to the Sign Up page, where she puts in her information
  • After she saves her information, a message with a button pops up that says "Get Started!"
  • She clicks the button and is taken to the User Interaction/Dashboard page
  • Angela begins to set up folders by clicking the "New Folder" icon
  • The browser prompts her for a folder name
  • Angela names the folder and clicks "Enter"
  • The browser stores the information and displays the newly named folder
  • Angela begins scanning receipts to put into the folder
  • Angela would like to change some of her settings, so goes to the Account Management page
  • She clicks the "Edit" button and her contact information becomes editable
  • She edits her contact information and clicks "Save"
  • The browser stores her information
  • Angela is done scanning receipts for the day and clicks the log out button
  • She is redirected to the home page, ready for the next session
